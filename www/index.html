HOW TO PLAY

PIECES & MOVEMENT

Pieces move like standard chess: King (1 sq any dir), Queen (any dir slide), Rook (straight slide), Bishop (diagonal slide), Knight (L-shape), Pawn (forward, captures diagonal).

3D LEVELS

The board has 15 vertical levels stacked in a rhombus shape. The middle level (8) is full 8-wide; levels above and below get narrower. Pieces can move between levels:
- Pawns: move forward along X-axis, up/down one level (white up, black down)
- Rooks: slide along level axis
- Bishops: slide diagonally across levels
- Queens: slide any direction including across levels
- Kings: step one square in any 3D direction
- Knights: L-shapes that can cross one level

RHOMBUS SHAPE

The board narrows as you go away from the center level. Pieces on narrower levels have fewer horizontal squares. If a piece's X coordinate exceeds the level width, it cannot go there.

CONTROLS

- Click/tap a piece to select, then click a highlighted square to move
- Drag to orbit the camera, scroll/pinch to zoom
- Use camera buttons (TOP, SIDE, W.POV, B.POV) for preset views
- Use the level slider to isolate specific levels

WINNING

Capture the enemy King or achieve Checkmate. Stalemate is a draw.
