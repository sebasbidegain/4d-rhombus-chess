# 4D Rhombus Chess — Short-Video Content Playbook

Ready-to-shoot short-form video concepts for TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts.
Every concept can be captured with a screen recording of the existing game — no advanced editing required.

---

## Universal rules (apply to every video)

- **Vertical 9:16**, 7–15 seconds unless noted.
- **Hook in frame 1** — big readable on-screen text, top third, high contrast.
- **Movement in the first 0.5s** — open on the board already rotating or a piece already moving. Never open on a static menu.
- **Loop it** — end on a frame that matches the opening so it replays seamlessly (boosts watch-time).
- **One idea per video** — sell one moment, don't explain the whole game.
- **CTA** — "Free — link in bio" end card, 1s max.
- **Cross-post** the same clip to TikTok + Reels + Shorts.

## 1. "Wait, chess isn't flat?" (best all-rounder)
- **Platform:** all · **Length:** 8s
- **Hook text (0s):** "You've been playing chess wrong your whole life"
- **Beats:** Normal-looking board → camera pulls back to reveal 15 stacked levels → a bishop slides diagonally up three levels.
- **Shot list:** ① Zoom in on middle level, looks like normal chess (1s). ② SIDE camera preset + scroll-zoom out, the rhombus tower reveals (2s). ③ Select a bishop, tap a highlighted square on a higher level, watch it travel (3s). ④ End card (1s).
- **Caption:** "3D chess where pieces move between levels. Free in your browser 👀 #chess #3d #webgl #indiegame"
- **Audio:** "wait for it" / plot-twist trending sound.

## 2. Popeye nostalgia bomb

- **Hook text:** "They made a chess set out of POPEYE 🫒"

- **Shot list:** ① Orbit start on Spinach Can pawns, text "Pawns = spinach cans" (2s). ② Pan to Bluto (rook), Wimpy w/ burger (bishop), Olive Oyl (queen), quick label each (5s). ③ End on Popeye the King, pipe + crossed arms, slow zoom (2s). ④ End card.

- **Audio:** upbeat retro-cartoon sound.

## 3. Greek gods flex

## 4. Satisfying theme-morph (highest loop potential)
- **Platform:** all · **Length:** 6s, looping
- **Hook text:** "Pick your army"
- **Beats:** Rapid theme switching, whole board transforms every ~0.7s.
- **Shot list:** One continuous take — press THEME repeatedly: Trooper → Robot → Crystal → Medieval → Alien → Popeye → Greek → back to Trooper. Keep the camera slowly orbiting so it's never static.
- **Caption:** "7 armies, one board. Which is yours? #satisfying #chess #3d"
- **Audio:** beat-drop / transition sound, each switch landing on the beat.

## 5. "POV: you're losing" comedy

## 6. The dev / builder angle (for Reddit + Hacker News + Shorts)
- **Platform:** YouTube Shorts + Reels · **Length:** 15s
- **Hook text:** "I built chess in 3D with 15 levels"
- **Beats:** Fast montage of the tech.
- **Shot list:** ① Board spinning (2s). ② Drag the level slider to peel levels away one by one (4s). ③ Cycle camera presets TOP → SIDE → W.POV (3s). ④ A knight doing an L-shape across a level (3s). ⑤ "Runs in your browser. No download." end card (3s).
- **Caption:** "Made with Three.js. Free, runs in-browser, open source. #webdev #threejs #javascript #gamedev"
- **Audio:** low-key lofi/techy sound.
- **Note:** Companion clip for a "Show HN" / r/WebGames post. Deliberately quieter tone than the others.

## 7. Interactive challenge (drives comments = reach)
- **Platform:** all · **Length:** 10s
- **Hook text:** "Find checkmate in this 3D board. I'll wait."
- **Beats:** Present a real puzzle position, hold, reveal the answer.
- **Shot list:** ① Slow orbit around a puzzle position, "White to move, mate in 1" text (4s). ② Freeze with countdown "3…2…1" (2s). ③ Play the winning move, "CHECKMATE" flashes (2s). ④ "Comment if you got it 👇" end card.
- **Caption:** "Hardest one yet. Did you find it? 👇 #chesspuzzle #chess #brainteaser"
- **Audio:** tense ticking-clock sound.

## Posting strategy

- **Batch-shoot** all raw footage in one sitting (orbits, theme cycles, a few real games, puzzle positions). Reuse clips across videos.
- **Post 1/day for 2 weeks**, mixing angles (novelty → satisfying → challenge → dev → comedy). Never post two of the same style back-to-back.
- **Double down on winners** — whichever concept overperforms in 48h, make 3 variants. That's also the concept to later hand a paid micro-influencer.
- **Pin your best video** to your profile once you have a store link.

> Chess/gaming audiences and developer audiences reward opposite things — epic hooks + trending sounds vs. quiet "I built this" competence. Concept #6 fishes the dev pond with the same footage; keep its tone separate from the rest.
