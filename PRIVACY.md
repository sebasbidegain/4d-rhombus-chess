# Privacy Policy — 4D Rhombus Chess

**Last updated: 13 June 2026**

4D Rhombus Chess ("the app", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. In short: **the game runs entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.**

---

## 1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The game itself does **not** collect, store on a server, or share any of the following:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, address)
- Location data
- Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics or behavioural tracking

The game contains **no third-party advertising SDKs and no analytics SDKs.**

---

## 2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app saves the following **only on your own device**, using the browser/WebView `localStorage` mechanism. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Saved games | Lets you resume a game later |
| Win/loss record | Shows your personal statistics |
| Theme & difficulty preferences | Remembers your chosen settings |
| Tutorial-seen flag | Avoids showing the tutorial repeatedly |

You can erase all of this at any time by clearing the app's data/storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

---

## 3. Optional Admin Analytics (Not Part of the Player Experience)

The project includes an **optional, separate** administrative dashboard (`admin.html`) intended only for the app operator. It is **not** part of the player-facing game and is not bundled into normal gameplay. If an operator chooses to configure it with their own Firebase project, it may store aggregate game statistics under that operator's control. Players are never asked for credentials and no player personal data is sent to it during normal play.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, the app is safe for general audiences.

---

## 5. Network Access

The app loads its fonts from Google Fonts the first time it runs (in browser/desktop builds). The packaged mobile apps are designed to run offline and do not require a network connection to play. The Android build explicitly blocks cleartext (non-HTTPS) network traffic.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised and the new version will be published in the project repository.
